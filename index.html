About Us

Phlux is the combined effort of Sarah Wylie and Allegra Anka. We're full stack engineers living and working in Philadelphia. We're New York Code + Design Academy bootcamp grads who know that sharpening your skills and building your network is a full-time commitment, and integral to your success as a developer.

That's the inspiration behind Phlux: community and skills building with like-minded folks in the Philadelphia tech scene. We're working on designing a range of programming dedicated to helping new developers feel more connected, and more prepared, in the job search and beyond.

We're doing an event called Whiteboard Wednesdays, where you can come to practice (or if you're shy, start by watching others practice) doing code challenges on the whiteboard in a simulated technical interview environment. Join us on July 18 for our first ever Whiteboard Wednesday, and check back on our events page every so often to see what we're up to!
